# Interview Readiness for E-commerce Analytics Project

## Common Interview Questions

1. **What metrics would you track for an e-commerce website?**  
   You should track metrics like conversion rate, average order value, customer acquisition cost, and customer lifetime value.

2. **How would you analyze customer behavior on an e-commerce site?**  
   Analyze customer behavior using web analytics tools to track user actions, segments, and A/B testing.

3. **Can you explain the importance of A/B testing in e-commerce?**  
   A/B testing allows you to compare two versions of a webpage or feature to determine which performs better, thereby optimizing sales.

4. **What data analysis tools are you proficient in?**  
   Tools such as Python, R, SQL, Google Analytics, and Tableau are commonly used for data analysis in e-commerce.

5. **How do you use customer segmentation in analytics?**  
   Customer segmentation helps target specific groups of customers based on behavior, demographics, or purchasing patterns, enhancing the marketing strategy.

## Preparing Your Answers
- Practice your responses to these questions in a concise and clear manner.
- Include relevant examples from past projects to illustrate your experience and skills.

## Additional Advice
- Stay updated with the latest e-commerce trends.
- Tailor your answers to the specific company and role you are applying to.
